Who We Are

Travel that gives back

A Sarajevo-based social enterprise sharing the authentic heart of Bosnia-Herzegovina.

Bosnia remains one of Europe's best-kept secrets — a land of medieval fortresses, Ottoman bazaars, and rivers of impossible clarity. We've spent over fifteen years building journeys that connect travellers to places most never discover.

All our profits support vulnerable communities across the country. When you travel with us, you leave something behind that lasts far longer than your visit.

Culture & Events

Sarajevo's cultural calendar

Annual · Summer
Jazz Fest Sarajevo
An independent non-profit festival drawing musicians from the USA, France, Germany, Norway and the UK — one of the region's most celebrated musical gatherings.
Music
Annual · Autumn
MESS Theatre Festival
Celebrating over 60 years, MESS is the longest-running and largest theatre festival in the Balkans. International productions and local premieres alike.
Theatre
Annual · Spring
Pravo Ljudski Film Festival
A film festival devoted entirely to human rights, running since 2006. Unique, thought-provoking cinema from filmmakers across the globe.
Film
Annual · Autumn
Ballet Fest & ZVRK Dance
Ballet's remarkable comeback in post-war Sarajevo, alongside ZVRK — the international contemporary dance festival that began in 2008.
Dance
